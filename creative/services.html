Systimax

Avaya’s Connectivity Solutions group, with its world renowned research establishment, Avaya Labs, delivers connectivity solutions to meet customers’ communication infrastructure needs with industry-leading performance, reliability, ease of management and lower overall cost of ownership.
Avaya’s solutions include:

  • SYSTIMAX® SCS - a full range of cabling solutions for enterprise networks, often referred to as LANs (Local Area Networks).
  • ExchangeMAX® - an extensive suite of cabling infrastructure solutions for service provider networks, typically deployed in Central Offices and customer premises.
  • Integrated Cabinet Solutions - a range of indoor and outdoor communication cabinets for switching, access and wireless equipment.
